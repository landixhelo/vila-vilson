A Few Words About Vila

VILA VILSON

Located in Sarandë and with Saranda City Beach reachable within 200 metres, Vila Vilson provides a garden, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi and a bar. This 3-star hotel offers dry cleaning services. The hotel features family rooms.

At the vila, all rooms include a wardrobe. Each room is fitted with air conditioning, a desk and a flat-screen TV, and certain rooms at Vila Vilson have a balcony. Guest rooms will provide guests with a fridge
